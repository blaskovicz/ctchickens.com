Why Join Our Community?

Connecticut Backyard Chickens is the go-to community for chicken enthusiasts across the state. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced keeper, you'll find support, advice, and friendship here.

Active Community

Connect with over 12,000 fellow chicken keepers in Connecticut. Get answers to your questions quickly from experienced members.

Expert Advice

Learn from experienced chicken keepers. Get tips on everything from coop building to health issues and egg production.

Buy & Sell Locally

Find supplies, equipment, and other chicken-related products from local members. Support your local chicken-keeping community.
